Who We Are

Our Story

We started working on Rent Wheels as our Graduate Level project for University of Texas at Dallas. The site is designed to be a dynamic website for providing Cars on Rent to Customers. This site will provide easy and fast booking options through its different modules. Various functionalities like “Select car by Location”, “”Select Car by type (SUV, Sedan, Hatchback” or “Select by Availability Date” have been implemented on the site for smooth booking experience. The site has attractive Front end for the Visitors and the Back end for administration of the site.

Our History

  • 2016  -

  • In 2016, we met as graduate students at UT Dallas. We formed a team for our Project for the course WPL and from there on the story of Rent Wheels started.
